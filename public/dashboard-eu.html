EU 90-Day Compliance Roadmap

A structured plan built around the EU's 2026 regulatory deadlines — CBAM registration, PPWR packaging, ESPR unsold goods, Pay Transparency HR audits, and CSRD data collection.

Phase-by-Phase Plan

  • 1

    Baseline Operational Audit

    Map your energy, water, waste and emission data against applicable thresholds. Identify which frameworks legally apply to your business.

    Week 1–2
  • 2

    Appoint Compliance Officers

    Register a certified Energy Manager (NEA) and Water Efficiency Manager if applicable. Set up internal reporting cadences.

    Week 2–4
  • 3

    Data Collection & Systems

    Install submetering where required. Begin collecting Scope 1 & 2 emission data. Set up packaging tonnage tracking systems.

    Week 4–8
  • 4

    Submit Mandatory Reports

    File annual energy & water reports to NEA. Submit packaging 3R plans. Register for Carbon Tax if applicable.

    Week 8–10
  • 5

    Build Your ESG Strategy

    Move from compliance to competitive advantage. Set reduction targets, engage stakeholders, and prepare for ISSB climate disclosures.

    Week 10–12+

Key Milestones

Immediate (0–30 days) Register with NEA, identify applicable thresholds, appoint compliance leads.
Short-term (30–60 days) Deploy metering infrastructure, start data collection, submit first reports.
Strategic (60–90 days) Set reduction targets, prepare ISSB climate disclosures, build investor narrative.

EC: CSDDD Due Diligence Guide
Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive — applies to >5,000 employees and >€1.5B turnover. Transposition by July 2027; full application July 2028.
Official Directive

PPWR: Packaging Compliance Guide
Key requirements effective August 12, 2026 — 100% recyclable packaging, PFAS ban in food-contact packaging, and digital marking (QR codes/pictograms).
Compliance Guide

ESPR unsold goods destruction ban (July 19, 2026), Digital Product Passport rollout timeline, and sector-specific ecodesign requirements for textiles and electronics.
